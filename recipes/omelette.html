Veggie Cheese Omelette Recipe

An omelette is a dish made by beating eggs and cooking them in a frying pan, often folded around a filling such as cheese, vegetables, herbs, or meats. It’s quick to prepare, customizable, and commonly served for breakfast. The texture ranges from soft and fluffy to firm, depending on cooking style.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion
  • Paprika pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them until well mixed.
  2. Finely chop the onion and paprika pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil and a small amount of butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  4. Sauté the chopped vegetables until soft.
  5. Pour the beaten eggs over the vegetables in the pan.
  6. Add a dessert spoon full of Parmesan Cheese
  7. Cook until the bottom is set, then fold the omelette in half.
  8. Cook for another minute, then slide onto a plate and serve hot.
